About Riza

Born in Pekalongan, Indonesia. Riza Akbar first picked up a camera at the early age of twelve - influenced by street and conceptual photography. Growing up with the massive development of social media and digital techniques enabled Riza to see the bigger picture what social media needed. After graduating high school he naturally felt the transition to fashion photography.

Now His focus is Portrait photography especially catalog and modelling, but He also take the graduation, sport, and street photography.

He currently resides in Jakarta and regularly travels for assignments, working between Jakarta, Tangerang, and Bogor.
